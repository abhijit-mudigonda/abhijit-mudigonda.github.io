Hello! I'm Abhijit, a rising senior at MIT, studying mathematics and theoretical computer science. I also love singing and arranging a cappella music, and am part of the [MIT Ohms][ohms-site]. When I'm not doing math or music, I spend my days running, cooking, climbing, and reading. 


I've found that putting ideas in words, spoken or written, helps me orient them better in my head. **pravāham** is an attempt at getting myself to do this more regularly. 


This site was built using [Github Pages][github-pages] with [Jekyll][jekyll-site] as a static site generator. I also use [tex2markdown][tex2markdown] to automatically convert my LaTeX files into reasonably pretty Markdown.
