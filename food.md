I reaaaaaallly enjoy eating good food, and I've been lucky enough to eat lots of it over the years. I decided that I should start a list of some of my favorite culinary experiences for posterity. Someday I will add photos. 

- Dosa from [Ram ki Bandi][ram-ki-bandi] in the Nampally area of Hyderabad, India (December 2018)
- Manet gelato from Suso in Venice (January 2019)
- Empanadas (especially the Campo flavor) from Emporio Andino in San Pedro de Atacama, Chile (August 2019)
- Rainbow Noodles from YoMa in Boston (November 2019)
- Chilaquiles from La Contenta in NYC (November 2019)
- Ulavacharu Biriyani from Ulavacharu in Sunnyvale (December 2019)
- Khao Soi Tofu - Khao San in the Bethany area of Portland (December 2019)

[ram-ki-bandi]: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U_-BgNO6XA8
